# **App Name**: FoodFetch

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Profiles: Enable secure user registration, login, and profile management for storing essential details like addresses and contact information.
- Restaurant & Menu Browsing: Allow users to explore a diverse range of restaurants and their full menus, complete with item descriptions, prices in Rupees, and high-quality images.
- Interactive Shopping Cart: Provide a dynamic shopping cart experience where users can easily add, remove, and update quantities of food items, with real-time price calculation.
- Secure Checkout Process: Guide users through a streamlined checkout flow, including delivery address selection, payment option integration (mock), and final order placement.
- Order Management & History: Allow users to track the status of their active orders and review their past order history for convenience and re-ordering.
- Personalized Recommendations: An AI tool that suggests food items and restaurants to users based on their browsing history and previous order patterns to enhance discoverability.
- Dynamic Search & Filters: Implement an efficient search functionality and filtering options by cuisine type, dietary preferences, or restaurant ratings.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, fresh green (#4AB814) to symbolize health and energy, providing strong contrast on light backgrounds.
- Background color: A very light, subtly desaturated green (#F1F5F0) to maintain a clean and airy feel while aligning with the primary hue.
- Accent color: A lively orange (#F5993D) for calls-to-action, alerts, and other interactive elements, ensuring high visibility and dynamism.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and highly readable qualities, suitable for all text types in a content-rich app.
- Use clean, contemporary vector icons that clearly represent common actions (e.g., cart, user, search) and food categories, aligning with the app's fresh aesthetic.
- Employ a mobile-first, grid-based responsive layout with card-style elements for restaurants and menu items, ensuring easy navigation and a decluttered interface.
- Incorporate subtle, fast-paced animations for interactive elements such as adding items to the cart or confirming an action, providing responsive visual feedback.
